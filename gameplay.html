Gameplay Overview and Videos

You can find openings by being aggressive and dodging into him. You can parry using blasphemous claw if you have it. You can use it during 3 specific moves when the sword is glowing gold. The pile driver from when he jumps down from the pillar. His slash after 3 air spin move. Pile driver after 2 air spin move. My advice is to stick close and get in attacks while dodging. When he does his overhand swings as long as you are next to his body the swings will miss. When he digs his sword into the ground stay away from the front. Go to the side or behind him while avoiding the explosion. If you stay at the front he will front flip into destined death. If you dodge away from his swings and don't stick next to him, he will use his front flip attacks. Dodge towards him and not away. Attack after dodging, you can get maybe one or two slash and prepare to dodge again. Again dodge towards him and attack. If he backsteps high ready roll towards him once past the swing and attack. If he backsteps low ready, roll twice to his left or jump over his spin and attack.

strategies, level reqs, and tips for Maliketh

For me... Bloodhound Fang (maxed), Mimic tear, heavy armor, golden order incantations for dps increase and defense.

Before getting to this point, players will naturally be at a higher level, considering this fight is nearing the end of the game. Regardless, try to aim to be at least level 130, ideally 150 with maxed weapons. It's also important to note that Maliketh will constantly try to diminish any gap between himself and the player, making ranged weapons obsolete if you're fighting alone.

Don't feel bad about using summons. Anyone who says using an Ash Summon means you didn't beat the boss is an elitist. Mimic Tear is gonna be one of your best bets even after the nerfs. I've had a lot of success with using Scarlet Rot against him. It's a pain to find an opening, but if you can tag him with it, Scarlet Rot will deal about 40% of his health bar. And his resistances reset between phases. Use the columns to block his projectiles and some of his movement. His melee attacks can extend just past them though, so watch for that. The range he has in Beast Cleric form is pretty limited. Make use of that to conserve as many heals as you can.
